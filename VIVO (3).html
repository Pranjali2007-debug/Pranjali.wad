VIVO

Vivo is a major Chinese tech company known for stylish, camera-focused smartphones with strong performance, offering various series (X, V, T, Y) for different users, featuring innovations like OIS cameras, fast charging, and 5G, with a strong presence in India where many phones are made locally.

Company Overview

  • Founded: 2009 by Shen Wei
    • .
  • Headquarters: Dongguan, China.
  • Products: Smartphones, software, accessories, focusing on camera tech, design, and battery.
  • Subsidiaries: iQOO.
  • Global Reach: Operates in over 100 countries, strong in India.

    • ♦Key Features and Technology

    Cameras: Known for high-megapixel sensors, OIS (Optical Image Stabilization), and unique portrait modes (e.g., Wedding Style Portrait).

    Design: Stylish designs, color-changing technology (Chameleon Glass).

    Performance: Good processors, long battery life, and fast charging (FlashCharge).

    Connectivity: Early adopter and contributor to 5G standards, with many 5G models available.

    Popular Series & Models (Examples)

    • X Series: Flagship, high-end camera phones (X200 Pro, X Fold 3 Pro).
    • V Series: Mid-range, stylish, camera-centric (V60, V60e, V40).
    • T Series: Performance-focused, often gaming-oriented (T4 Pro, T4x).
    • Y Series: Budget-friendly, long battery, good features (Y300 5G, Y19s).

    In IndiaStrong market presence with a commitment to local manufacturing ("made in India") for phones sold in India. Offers affordable financing options like Bajaj Finserv EMI. For specific details, visit the vivo India website.
