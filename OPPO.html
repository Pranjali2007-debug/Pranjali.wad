OPPO

OPPO is a major global smartphone brand known for innovative features, strong camera tech (Hasselblad partnership), fast charging (SUPERVOOC), and stylish designs, offering various series like flagship Find X, mid-range Reno/F, and budget-friendly A/K series, focusing on high performance, durability, and long battery life with its ColorOS software. They're recognized for user-centric tech, rapid growth, and expanding global presence, emphasizing features like advanced processors (Dimensity 9400), large batteries (7000mAh), and robust build quality with high IP ratings.

Budget-friendly, robust phones with large batteries and high refresh rate displays.
